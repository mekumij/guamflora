About this guide
Welcome to the DPRS Garden!

Here, we have created a digital archive of the vibrant flora and fungi that define Guam's ecosystem. This archive pulls from community-driven observations on iNaturalist into an accessible guide for any person interested in learning about Guam's biodiversity. Whether you are an avid hiker, a student of the natural world, or a curious neighbor, this resource is designed to help you identify, understand, and protect the unique species that share our island home. Enjoy your stay :)
