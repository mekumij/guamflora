About This Guide

Welcome to the Official DPRS Botanical and Mycological Repository. Here, we have created a digital archive of the vibrant flora and fungi that can be found in Guam’s ecosystems. Thanks to the community-driven observations on iNaturalist, we have created an accessible guide for any person interested in learning about Guam’s biodiversity. Whether you are an avid hiker, a student, or a curious neighbor, this resource was created to help you identify, understand, and protect the unique species that share our island home. Enjoy your stay! :)

Do you want to learn more about the hidden biodiversity that can be found in your backyard? Ever see a plant that looked interesting but you had no idea what it was? Sign-up on iNaturalist to join other Guam citizens in documenting our island’s biodiversity!
